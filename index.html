Robert Short for President 2024

Our Mission

We are running to rebuild America's economy, safeguard national security, and ensure equality for all Americans while putting America first.

Key Policies

Economy

We will focus on rebuilding America's manufacturing sector, lowering taxes for the middle class, and creating millions of new jobs to ensure a strong and resilient economy.

Healthcare

Our goal is to reform the healthcare system to ensure affordable and accessible healthcare for every American, without adding unnecessary financial burdens to the people.

National Security

We will strengthen border security, maintain a robust military presence, and work closely with our international allies to protect our nation from external threats.

Equality for All

Equality is a cornerstone of our platform. We believe in providing equal rights and opportunities for all citizens, ensuring fairness in education, employment, and healthcare.

America First

Our America First policy focuses on putting the needs of American citizens at the forefront of all our policies, from foreign relations to domestic economic reform.

Support for Innovation

We will invest in technological innovation, clean energy, and infrastructure development to ensure America remains at the forefront of global progress and leadership.
