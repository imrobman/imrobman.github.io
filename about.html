About Robert Short

Robert Short is a passionate leader, deeply committed to rebuilding America. With a background in public service and a strong belief in the values that made America great, Robert is running for President in 2024 to lead the country through unprecedented times with a focus on national security, economic growth, and equal opportunities for all.

Robert has spent years advocating for the people, with a clear focus on practical solutions that address the real challenges Americans face today, including healthcare reform, tax relief, and innovation in technology and infrastructure.

His belief in the American people drives his campaign, and he stands firmly on the platform that "Time is Short! Save America NOW!"
