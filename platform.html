Robert Short's Platform

Economy

Our economy is in dire need of reform. Robert Short believes in bringing jobs back to the United States, reducing unnecessary government spending, and lowering taxes for the middle class.

Healthcare

Healthcare should be affordable and accessible to every American. Robert is committed to reforming healthcare to ensure every citizen receives the medical attention they need without going into debt.

National Security

Robert Short stands for a strong, well-defended nation. We must protect our borders, maintain a robust military, and ensure our allies know we stand with them against threats to freedom.

Equality

Robert is a proud advocate for equality. Every American, regardless of race, gender, or sexual orientation, deserves equal rights and opportunities to succeed in life.
