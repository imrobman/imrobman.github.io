Robert Short's Platform

Economy

We are committed to rebuilding America's economy by creating millions of new jobs, revitalizing our manufacturing sector, lowering taxes for the middle class, and reducing government waste.

Healthcare

Healthcare should be affordable and accessible for all. We plan to reform healthcare to eliminate unnecessary costs and ensure that every American has access to the care they need without going into debt.

National Security

National security is a top priority. We will strengthen our borders, maintain a strong military, and work closely with our allies to protect our nation from external threats.

Equality for All

Every American deserves equal rights and opportunities. We will work to ensure fairness in education, employment, healthcare, and civil rights for every citizen, regardless of race, gender, or sexual orientation.

America First

Our America First policy puts the needs of American citizens above all else. We believe in prioritizing American workers, businesses, and families in all aspects of government policy and foreign relations.

Innovation and Infrastructure

We are committed to investing in clean energy, technological innovation, and infrastructure development to keep America competitive in the global market and ensure a prosperous future for generations to come.
